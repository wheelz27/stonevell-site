Stonevell Arena Product

Hard Knocks Arena

Core hook: Could they do it again? Strip away money, fame, and network. Keep only skill, judgment, and work ethic.

Non-negotiable rule: If it did not happen in real life, it does not count.

Watch Comebacks Play Rebuild Mode Predict Outcomes View Leaderboard

1) Live-Game Rules (Reality Locked)

What they lose

  • Old network and warm intros
  • Fame leverage and media shortcuts
  • Investor friends and hidden capital
  • Existing team and customer list

What they keep

  • Skills and industry knowledge
  • Decision style and risk tolerance
  • Work ethic and emotional profile
  • Cold-start execution ability

Each card tracks: starting cash, skill edge, weakness, reputation, risk tolerance, decision style, and recovery strategy.

3) Show Modes & Viewer Experience

Watch ModePlay ModePrediction ModeVersus Mode
  • Watch: real calls, pitches, offers, deals, failures, pivots, and receipts.
  • Play: users run the same cold-start challenge and compare results.
  • Predict: users guess weekly outcomes and earn points/credits.
  • Versus: two personas rebuild in parallel with same constraints.

4) Game Loop (Week by Week)

  1. Pick action: calls, door knocks, content, offer, partnership, hire, or cost cuts.
  2. Commit time and budget from limited resources.
  3. Log verified outcomes (proof links, invoices, contracts).
  4. Resolve random event (lawsuit, referral, churn, crash, burnout, tax hit).
  5. Update score and next week’s odds.

5) Scoring, Odds, and Prediction Questions

Track cash, monthly revenue, profit, debt, reputation, team strength, audience size, assets, stress, and runway.

  • Will they survive the month?
  • Will they hit first $10K?
  • Will they take debt?
  • Hire or stay solo?
  • Who rebuilds fastest?

6) Edge Intel Integration

Edge Intel powers live dashboards, decision odds, persona performance, user predictions, and leaderboard rankings.

MVP scope: 8 personas, one 12-week scenario, one city template, core leaderboard, premium placeholders.
