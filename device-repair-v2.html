What We Fix

If it has a screen or a motherboard, we can probably fix it.

We work on laptops, desktops, tablets, and phones — all brands, all ages. Whether it's a cracked screen on a MacBook Air or a virus-ridden Windows desktop from 2015, we'll diagnose it for free and tell you exactly what it'll cost before we touch anything.

Slow / Freezing Computer

Takes 5 minutes to boot and locks up when you open Chrome. Could be a dying hard drive, too little RAM, bloatware, or malware. We'll find it and fix it.

Typical cost: $75 – $150

Cracked or Dead Screen

Laptop, tablet, or phone screen shattered or showing lines. We source quality replacement parts and handle the swap. Most laptop screens replaced same day.

Typical cost: $100 – $250

Virus & Malware

Pop-ups everywhere, browser hijacked, fake antivirus warnings. We nuke the infection, clean the system, install real protection, and show you how to avoid it next time.

Typical cost: $75 – $125

Won't Turn On / Blue Screen

Dead on arrival, stuck in boot loop, blue screen of death. Could be hardware failure, corrupted OS, or a bad update. We diagnose the actual cause — not just the symptom.

Typical cost: $75 – $200

Data Recovery

Accidentally deleted files, crashed hard drive, corrupted USB. We recover what we can from mechanical drives, SSDs, and flash storage. If we can't get it back, you don't pay.

Typical cost: $100 – $300

Hardware Upgrades

Your machine is fine — it just needs more muscle. SSD upgrade, RAM expansion, GPU swap. We'll tell you if an upgrade makes sense or if it's time for a new machine.

Typical cost: $50 – $175 + parts

WiFi & Network Issues

Dead zones, constant drops, can't connect, slow speeds. We diagnose whether it's your device, your router, or your ISP — and fix what's actually broken.

Typical cost: $75 – $150

New Device Setup

Just bought a new laptop or desktop? We'll transfer your files, install your software, set up email, configure security, and make sure everything works.

Typical cost: $75 – $125
